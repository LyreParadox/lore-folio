Sired by Czechoslovakian import CH Cheeky Ladior SchH III, IPO I out of CH Classic v d Allenhof CD, BH this bitch possesses the best European working Giant bloodlines available in the United States today. Onyx combines the imported blood of three of the finest Giant Schnauzers in US competition in the past 10 years and is proving to produce top quality working dogs in her own right. Her pedigree combines the German lines of Giebiko, Bulowshohe and Haus Fortuna which makes this cross so exceptional. In 2020 Onyx completed her AKC CDX title in 3 consecutive trials and won High in Trial at the Giant Schnauzer Club of America Midwest Specialty. She has also been titled with her Schutzhund obedience BH. She was awarded her Canine Good Citizen in May of 2023 and passed her AD endurance title and was also awarded a V rating in a class of 9 bitches at the Working Schnauzer Federation National in Charlotte, N.C. in October, 2023 .
